Student GIS & Cartography Portfolio

This portfolio is a collection of the maps I created during Geographical Information Systems Summer Lab at Utrecht University. Geographical Information Systems (GIS) are softwares processing and integrating real world data and connecting them to spatial and temporal coordinates. Things like weather forecast, Google Maps, or location based services all use GIS to function. GIS science is the study of GIS and its possible applications. In the GIS Summer Lab through 6 practical assignments I have learned about data collection and processing, GIS analysis, and visualization of results. The course also provided me with tachnical skills to create this web portfolio using GitHub.
